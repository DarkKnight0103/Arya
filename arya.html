About Us

Two partners, Harry and Stephanie, decided to open a dream company for sending flowers and gifts. Harry is a flower farmer, ensuring the best flowers are used for the perfect occasions. Stephanie, on the other hand, is an expert in choosing the perfect gift for different occasions. Both are business-minded with the goal of making the world a happier place to be. The business started 2 months ago, but it seems the local neighborhood finds it difficult to transact with us as we operate from a small shop on the outskirts of town, close to the flower farm.
